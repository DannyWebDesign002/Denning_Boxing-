Our Story

BUILT ON
HEART &
SOUL


Founded in 2006 by Peter Stokes, Denning Boxing Club has been the heartbeat of East Bunbury's boxing community for two decades — one of the most beloved community gyms in Western Australia.

We've inspired kids, women, beginners, and seasoned fighters to lace up, push limits, and find something real. Every bag, every session, every laugh — built with heart.
